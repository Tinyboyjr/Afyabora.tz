About Us

AFYA BORA NUTRITION ORGANIZATION

Is the organization located in TANZANIA. The organization was founded in June 2021 by a team of student from SOKOINE UNIVERSITY OF AGRICULTURAL in Department of Food, Technology and Consumer Science. The primary aim is to solve different nutritional problems facing the community especially young children, women of reproductive age and elderly and to equip its members with knowledge and practices of nutrition and counseling skills.

MISSION

The mission of AFYA BORA NUTRITION ORGANIZATION is to better human health by spreading the fundamental knowledge of food and nutrition to the members and community at large.

VISION

AFYA BORA NUTRITION ORGANIZATION serves the community through provision of nutrition education which contributes to good health and prevention of disease.
